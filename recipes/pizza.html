Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the dough on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce, sprinkle cheese, and add toppings.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
